This demonstration shows how the new conflict prevention system works to prevent overlapping bookings between pending and approved room reservations.

Available

Room is completely available for booking at the selected time.

Pending Conflict

Room has pending booking(s) that may conflict. Booking allowed with warning.

Approved Conflict

Room has approved booking(s) that conflict. Booking blocked completely.
